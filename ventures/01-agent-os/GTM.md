# GTM

## Product entry
Start with a narrow offer for enterprises that already use agents in internal workflows.

## Initial promise
Give teams better control, execution trace and safer tool access for business agents.

## First customers
- medium and large companies with active AI pilots
- consultancies that deliver AI automation

## Best initial use cases
- agents that call internal APIs
- agents that read documents and trigger internal actions
- agents that work with sensitive business workflows

## Commercial direction
Do not sell a universal platform first.
Sell a control layer for risky agent operations.

## Good buying signals
- the company already has working pilots
- they care about audit and visibility
- they had at least one hard-to-diagnose agent incident

## Bad signals
- they only want a futuristic demo
- they have no real agents yet
- nobody owns the budget or the risk
