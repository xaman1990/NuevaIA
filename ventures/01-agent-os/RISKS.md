# Risks

## Product risks
- scope too wide
- weak wedge
- buyers like the idea but do not buy soon

## Technical risks
- too much platform before validation
- high support load
- poor handling of tool permissions

## Business risks
- long enterprise cycles
- low urgency in some accounts
- large vendors move faster

## Operating risks
- onboarding takes too long
- cost per customer is too high
- pilots do not convert

## Main mitigation
Keep the first offer narrow and tied to one risky workflow.
