# Customers

## Who to interview first
- CTOs with active AI pilots
- Heads of AI or Automation
- Platform engineers supporting internal agents
- Security leaders worried about agent behavior

## Questions
1. What agents are already running?
2. Which of them touch important systems?
3. What is hard to see today when an agent runs?
4. What incident would make this urgent?
5. Who would approve a tool like this?
6. What would block purchase?

## Good answers
- We already have agents in internal workflows
- We do not fully trust what they do
- We need audit and better control
- We had at least one confusing incident

## Bad answers
- We are just exploring AI
- We do not have real workflows yet
- This feels interesting but not urgent
